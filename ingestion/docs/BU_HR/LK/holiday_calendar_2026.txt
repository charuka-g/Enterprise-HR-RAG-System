OVERVIEW
==========================================================================

The following is the official list of public holidays applicable to all Meridian Holdings PLC employees working in Sri Lanka for the calendar year 2026. These holidays are in addition to your annual leave entitlement.

Employees required to work on a public holiday will receive overtime pay in accordance with the Overtime and Shift Policy (HR-LK-004) or a substitute day off, as agreed with their line manager.

Any changes to this calendar due to government gazette notifications will be communicated promptly by the HR department.

BUSINESS UNIT-SPECIFIC OBSERVANCES
==========================================================================

  All BUs:
  - Last working day before Sinhala & Tamil New Year (typically 12 April 2026):
    Half-day — employees may leave at 1:00 PM. Lunch will be served.

  - 26 December 2026 (Boxing Day): Retail stores remain open. Staff will
    receive double overtime for hours worked. Substitute holiday to be
    taken within 30 days.

==========================================================================
SUBSTITUTE HOLIDAYS
==========================================================================

Where a public holiday falls on a Sunday, the following Monday is treated as the substitute holiday (as per the government gazette). Where a public holiday falls on a Saturday (applicable to 5-day work week employees):
  - The preceding Friday will be treated as the substitute holiday, subject to official gazette notification.
  - HR will communicate changes promptly.

==========================================================================
WORKING ON PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
==========================================================================

Employees required to work on a public holiday must receive written pre-approval from their Business Unit Head. Compensation will be:

  Option A — Overtime Pay:
  - Two times (2x) the hourly basic rate for each hour worked on the public holiday

  Option B — Substitute Holiday:
  - One full day off in lieu, to be taken within 60 calendar days of the public holiday worked

The employee and manager must agree in writing (via ESS) which option applies. If no option is selected within 5 working days, Option B (substitute holiday) will apply by default.

==========================================================================
RELIGIOUS AND CULTURAL LEAVE BEYOND THE CALENDAR
==========================================================================

Meridian Holdings PLC respects the diversity of its workforce. Employees who observe religious or cultural occasions not listed in this calendar (e.g., Thai Pongal, Eid al-Fitr if not gazetted, Sinhalese Buddhist ceremonies) may apply for annual leave or unpaid leave for those days. Managers are encouraged to accommodate such requests wherever operationally feasible.
