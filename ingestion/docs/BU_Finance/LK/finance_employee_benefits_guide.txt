WELCOME
==========================================================================

This guide summarizes the full benefits package available to permanent employees of Meridian Finance (Pvt) Ltd, a subsidiary of Meridian Holdings PLC. Benefits are reviewed annually and subject to change; the most current version of this guide is always available on the ESS portal.

Benefits are in addition to your base salary and performance-linked incentives. This guide is for information purposes; the detailed terms of each benefit are governed by the relevant policy or insurance certificate.

==========================================================================
1. STATUTORY BENEFITS
==========================================================================

1.1 Employees' Provident Fund (EPF)

  Employee Contribution:   8% of gross salary
  Employer Contribution:  12% of gross salary

Contributions are remitted monthly to the Central Bank of Sri Lanka. Your EPF member number is issued within 30 days of joining.

1.2 Employees' Trust Fund (ETF)

  Employer Contribution:   3% of gross salary

The ETF provides additional financial security at the end of your service. Withdrawal rights are governed by the ETF Act.

1.3 Gratuity

Employees who complete 5 or more years of continuous service are entitled to gratuity under the Payment of Gratuity Act. Gratuity is calculated as:
  Gratuity = (Last drawn monthly salary ÷ 2) × Years of completed service

2. HEALTH AND MEDICAL BENEFITS
==========================================================================

2.1 Comprehensive Group Health Insurance

All permanent Meridian Finance employees and their immediate family members (spouse and up to 2 children under 18) are covered under the Meridian Group Health Insurance scheme, effective from the first day of the second month of service.

Coverage per insured per year:

  In-patient (hospitalization):
    - Employee:                        LKR 1,500,000
    - Spouse:                          LKR 1,000,000
    - Each child (up to 2):            LKR 750,000

  Out-patient (OPD) — Employee only:   LKR 75,000 per year
    - Covers: Consultations, prescribed medications, investigations
    - Co-payment: 20% per claim

  Critical Illness Cover (Employee):   LKR 2,000,000 lump sum
    - Covers: Cancer, heart attack, stroke, kidney failure, and 15 other listed conditions

  Dental Cover (Employee only):        LKR 30,000 per year
    - Covers: Scaling, fillings, extractions (excludes cosmetic dentistry)

  Vision Cover (Employee only):        LKR 20,000 per 2 years
    - Covers: Prescription spectacles, contact lenses (not LASIK)

Panel Hospitals: A list of panel hospitals is available on the HR portal. Cashless treatment is available at panel hospitals for in-patient claims. Out-of-pocket payments at non-panel hospitals will be reimbursed upon claim submission.

2.2 Annual Health Screening

All Meridian Finance employees are entitled to a free annual health screening at any of the Company's designated clinic partners. The screening includes a full blood count, lipid profile, blood glucose, blood pressure, BMI, and a consultation with a general practitioner. Screening is encouraged from age 30 and mandatory from age 40.

3. FINANCIAL BENEFITS
==========================================================================

3.1 13th Month Salary (Festival Advance / Bonus)

All confirmed employees receive a guaranteed 13th month salary paid in December, equivalent to one month's basic salary. This is separate from the performance bonus.

3.2 Annual Performance Bonus

Meridian Finance employees are eligible for an annual performance bonus based on:
  - Individual performance rating (from annual appraisal)
  - Business Unit financial performance against targets

The bonus range by rating:

  Outstanding (5):          Up to 25% of annual basic salary
  Exceeds Expectations (4): Up to 15% of annual basic salary
  Meets Expectations (3):   Up to 8% of annual basic salary
  Below Expectations (2):   No bonus
  Unsatisfactory (1):       No bonus

Bonuses are subject to BU profitability and are not guaranteed. Employees must be in service on the date of payment to receive the bonus.

3.3 Staff Loan Facility

Confirmed employees with at least 12 months of service may apply for an interest-free staff loan of up to 3 months' basic salary, repayable over 24 months via payroll deduction. Applications are subject to Finance department approval and an employee may hold only one staff loan at a time.

3.4 Salary Advance

In genuine emergencies, employees may apply for a one-time salary advance of up to 50% of the current month's net salary, repayable within 3 months. Requires line manager and Finance HR approval.

4. RETIREMENT PLANNING
==========================================================================

4.1 Meridian Finance operates a voluntary Supplementary Retirement Savings Plan for Grade M2 and above:
  - Employee contribution: 5% of basic salary (voluntary)
  - Company matching contribution: Up to 5% of basic salary
  - Funds are invested in a professionally managed unit trust portfolio
  - Vesting schedule: 25% vested after 2 years, 50% after 3 years, 100% after 5 years of participation

8. WELLBEING AND RECREATION
==========================================================================

8.1 Employee Assistance Programme (EAP)

All employees and their immediate family members have access to the Meridian EAP, which provides:
  - 6 free confidential counseling sessions per year (individual or family)
  - Financial coaching (2 sessions per year)
  - Legal advisory (1 session per year for personal legal queries)

EAP services are available 24/7 via: 1-800-EAP-MRDIAN or eap@meridianholdings.lk (responses within 24 hours).

8.2 Sports and Fitness

Meridian Finance contributes LKR 3,000 per month toward gym membership at any of the Company's partner gyms (a list is available on HR portal). Employees may also participate in Meridian Group sports teams (cricket, badminton, volleyball, and futsal).

8.3 Family Day and Annual Dinner

The Company organizes an annual Family Day and an Annual Dinner and Dance, both of which are fully funded. Spouses and children are welcome at Family Day.
